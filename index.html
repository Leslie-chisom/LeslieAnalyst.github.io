Sustainable Fashion Project

At the sustainability fair, I worked as a data analyst and utilized my skills to gather research on past auctions where items such as donated food and clothing were sold. My role involved collecting and analyzing data related to the items that were auctioned off, including the quantity, quality, and price of each item. I also looked at trends in the auction data over time, such as which items were most popular and which ones sold for the highest prices.
